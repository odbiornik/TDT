# Instructions for the Participant

### 1. Start Signal
The study will begin with a sequence of control lights: 
**red** → **yellow** → **green**. 
When you see the green light, it means the first stimuli will appear shortly.

### 2. Your Task
During the test, you will see very short white flashes. Your task is to decide:
* Did you see **one** flash?
* Did you see **two separate** flashes occurring in rapid succession?

### 3. Response Buttons
After each stimulus, press the corresponding button:
* 🔵 **Blue button** = I saw **TWO** separate flashes.
* 🔴 **Red button** = I saw **ONE** flash (or they merged into one).

### 4. Important Tips
* **This is not a reflex test:** There is no need to hurry. Accuracy and your genuine impression are what matter most.
* **Trust your intuition:** Respond naturally, based on your first impression. Do not over-analyze each flash.
* **Decide even if uncertain:** If you are not 100% sure, choose the option that seems more likely to you.

### 5. End of Study
The end of the test will be signaled by both lights turning **steady red**.
